Snow Onion Fried Chicken

Original crispy Korean fried chicken with sweet spring onions and creamy onion on top. The sauce is a mixture of mayonnaise, yogurt, crushed peanuts and a little bit of sugar. It may sound a little weird, but trust me, it's good. The crunchy fried chicken mixed with the sweet and cold onions pair so well once the sweet sauce is added.
