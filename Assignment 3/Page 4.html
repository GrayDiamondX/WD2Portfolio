Gimbap (김밥)

Korean gimbap/kimbap contain many different vegetables like carrots, cucumbers, bell peppers, spinach, and picked radish. For some balance, beef, spam, saucage, or egg is added into the roll. Dried seaweed can be found outside the roll, unlike Japanese maki rolls to keep everything inside. The rolls are sometimes brushed with seasame oil in order to keep them from sticking everywhere.
