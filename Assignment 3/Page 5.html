Ramyeon (라면)

Ramyeon, is made of curly dried noodles rehydrated in mild to spicy broth. While ingredients are similar to Ramen, our Ramyeon includes marinated and cooked pork, savory sausage, a little bit of seasame oil, furikake seasoning, and/or egg thats been cooked and scrambled in the broth. Super tasty!
