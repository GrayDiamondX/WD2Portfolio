Bibimbap (비빔밥)

One of Bibimbap's history is that it was created with the left over scraps people wanted to get rid of before New Years. The term "bibim" means to mix and "bap" means rice. Bibimbap is made of sautéed and seasoned vegetables, gochujian, sunny side up egg, and marinated meat (usually beef).
