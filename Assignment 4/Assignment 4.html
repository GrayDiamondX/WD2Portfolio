Morning Dove

"Is it a bug? Is it a super hero? No! It's a bird!"

We are Morning Dove, a bird watching organization that loves to explore the world of birds and teach others how to identify the variations of each bird species. Our goal is to take you on tours around your local parks or forest preserves and talk about what birds are in your area, as well as teaching new and fun facts along the way!

We encourage people of all ages to come join us! We welcome photographers, naturalists, conservationists, and bird lovers new and old!
