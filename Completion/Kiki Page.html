Kiki the Cupcake
January 23 - Age: Cupcake
ISFP - 2.8 inches (without frosting) (71.12 mm)

Home: Icey Creams
Gender: Non-binary




Represents: Creativity

The shortest of the group and lives furthest away (way up north). Loves to draw and play with their cousin, Poppy!


Kiki helps with getting your creative juices running! When you’re down, Kiki will pick up a pencil (no matter what size) and doodle something that might peak your interest. Kiki also encourages you to try a different medium! They know stepping out of your comfort zone is hard and scary, but they promise, they’ll be right by your side!

Kiki loves creating things from nothing and will encourage you to do the same! They love finding paper bags, candy wrappers, as well as other scraps lying around, and making them into items for scrapbooks, mementos, and collages! No wasting here!

Kiki represents my love of taking tossed items and sticking them into my own sketchbooks!
